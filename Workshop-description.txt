[h1]Playable Adepta Sororitas[/h1]
Makes the Adepta Sororitas Origin pickeable for your Rogue Trader at Game Start.
This might also enable you to recruit multiple Adepta Sororitas mercenaries.

[h2]Usage[/h2]
To use:
[olist]
[*][b]Before you start the character creation:[/b] Click the button in the Playable Adepta Sororitas mod section of the UnityModManager GUI.
[*]Turn the "Create Adepta Sororitas" toggle on.
[*]Now start creating a new character
[*]After finishing the character creation don't forget to turn the toggle back off.
[/olist]

[h2]Additional Information[/h2]
[list]
[*]Because the Adepta Sororitas Origin doesn't have any Darkest Hours and Moments of Triumph I've unlocked all of them for the Adepta Sororitas.
[/list]
